Welcome to IT School RTO

The school provides training and assessment resources related to Information and Communication Technology (ICT). Specifically, it covers training on generating basic client-side scripts, which is an important skill in the ICT field.

The school provides a range of learning resources, including the IT Works Student User Guide, to help students successfully complete assessment tasks. Students can also receive specific guidance on how to complete assessment tasks, including how to prepare and submit assessment works, as well as how to use assessment cover sheets.

With a team of experienced and passionate educators, we are committed to delivering engaging and interactive learning experiences. Whether you are a student looking to kickstart your IT career or an RTO seeking to enhance your training offerings, RTO Name has got you covered.
